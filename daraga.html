Daraga Church

  • Built by the Franciscans in 1772, the church is also known as "Our Lady of the Gate Parish Church" and overlooks the picturesque perfectly-coned shape of Mount Mayon. The church was also declared a National Cultural Treasure in 2007 
  • Also known as Nuestra Señora de la Porteria Church, Its baroque architecture is noitable, with volcanic rock facades and intricates carvings. The church served as a refuge during the eruption of Mount Mayon in 1814
