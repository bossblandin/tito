Mayon Volcano

  • At the heart of this region lies Mount Mayon, one of the main attractions of Bicol. It’s an iconic landmark that attracts visitors from all over the world. If you’re planning a trip to Bicol, here’s why you should make sure to visit Mayon Volcano.
  • Mayon Volcano is one of the most picturesque mountains in the world. Its symmetrical cone shape is a sight to behold, and the surrounding landscape is just as stunning. A visit here is a must for nature lovers, photographers, and beauty seekers alike.
  • It’s also a hotspot for adventure seekers. Hiking to the summit is a challenging and rewarding experience that offers breathtaking views. The hike can take up to 8 hours, but the effort is worth it.
  • For a more relaxed experience, take an ATV tour around the foothills or enjoy a helicopter ride for a bird’s eye view of the volcano.
  • In short, Mayon Volcano is a must-visit destination in Bicol, Philippines, offering unforgettable memories whether you're an adventurer or a traveler seeking unique experiences.
