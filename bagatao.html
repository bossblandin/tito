Bagatao Island Lighthouse

  • Bagatao Island Lighthouse, built in 1903 during the American period. Lighthouse located on Bagatao Island on the eastern side of the entrance to Sorsogon Bay, in the province of Sorsogon in the Bicol region. It was used and still being used to assist ships in navigating Ticao Pass together with Capul Light at the entrance to San Bernardino Strait and Bugui Point Light in northern Masbate. It is one of the historical lighthouse in the Philippines that still stands as a testament to maritime history.
